About Us

GUVI is an online learning platform that offers technology courses in vernacular languages like Hindi, Kannada, Tamil, Telugu, and Bengali. GUVI's courses are designed to be beginner-friendly and easy to learn, and they offer globally recognized certificates from top institutions like IIT-Madras.
